Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2025

Overview

SpotCheck helps you see how busy nearby places are before you go. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Information We Collect

  • Location Data: We access your device location to show nearby places and their crowd levels. Your exact location is never stored on our servers.
  • Email Address: If you create an account, we store your email for authentication only.
  • Crowd Reports: When you report how busy a place is, we store the place ID, crowd level, and timestamp. Reports are anonymous.
  • Anonymous ID: Guest users get an anonymous identifier that cannot be traced back to them.

How We Use Your Data

  • Nearby Places: Your location is sent to Google Places API to find places near you.
  • Crowd Estimates: We aggregate crowd reports to show estimated busyness levels.
  • Pattern Learning: Aggregated data improves predictions over time. No individual data is used.

Data Storage

  • Cloud Storage: Data is stored securely using Supabase with row-level security.
  • Local Storage: Some history data is stored on your device and never leaves it.
  • Data Retention: Crowd reports expire automatically. Local data is retained up to 90 days.

Third-Party Services

  • Google Places API — place discovery and business hours
  • Supabase — authentication and cloud storage
  • Apple MapKit — fallback place discovery

Your Rights

  • Delete your account and all data anytime from app settings.
  • Use the app without an account as a guest.
  • Revoke location access anytime in device Settings.

Children's Privacy

SpotCheck is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time and will update the date at the top.
